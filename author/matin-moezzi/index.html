Matin Moezzi

Computer Science Student

Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic)

I am a senior undergraduate student studying computer science at Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic). My goal is to develop intelligent agents that learn to optimally interact with stochastic environments. In order to achieve this, I mainly focus on Reinforcement Learning (RL), specifically multi-agent RL and Partially Observable MDPs. In addition, I am interested in studying Stochastic Processes and Stochastic Optimal Control.
I am currently working on solving control problems described by a system of differential equations via Deep RL methods as my bachelor’s thesis under the supervision of Prof. M. B. Menhaj at the Computational Intelligent & High Dimensional Systems Lab in the Electrical Engineering Department and Prof. M. Abbaszadeh.
