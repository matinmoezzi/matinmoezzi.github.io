About me

I am a master of applied science (MASc) student at the mechanical and industrial engineering department of the University of Toronto. I am currently working on transfer learning in reinforcement learning with dynamic mismatch under the supervision of Professor Chi-Guhn Lee. My research interests mainly focus on sequential decision-making problems formulated in the reinforcement learning framework.
