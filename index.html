About Me

As a 2nd-year M.A.Sc. student at the University of Toronto, my research aims to address two critical Reinforcement Learning challenges as applied to complex real-world environments: sample complexity and generalization using deep generative models.

Research Interests

  • Reinforcement Learning (RL): Model-based RL, Offline RL
  • Deep Generative Models: VAEs, Diffusion models, Score-based Generative Models
  • Imitation Learning (IL): Behavioral Cloning, Adversarial IL, Inverse RL, Sim-to-Real Transfer
