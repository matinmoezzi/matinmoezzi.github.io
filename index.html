About Me

I am a 2nd-year M.A.Sc. student at the University of Toronto, specializing in reinforcement learning (RL), deep generative models, and robot learning. My research is particularly aims to address two critical RL challenges as applied to robotics: sample complexity and generalization, under supervision of Prof. C.G. Lee

Research Interests

  • Reinforcement Learning (RL): Model-based RL, Offline RL
  • Deep Generative Models: VAEs, Diffusion models, Score-based Generative Models
  • Imitation Learning (IL): Sim-to-Real, Behavioral Cloning, Adversarial IL, Inverse RL
  • Robot Learning: MPC-based Planning
